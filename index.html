Frequently Asked Questions

What's up with the "placeholder tab stops" on your examples?

In some setups, the first tab stop on the page is not always read by the screen reader. NVDA + Firefox is one instance where this occurs. I add placeholder links at the top and bottom of each page to make texting content easier. They shouldn't be used for "real" content.
